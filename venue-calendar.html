🌟 How It Works

1

Set your weekly music schedule in My Schedule — the days of the week and times you book live music.

2

The app generates all upcoming occurrences of those days over the next 90 days as candidate dates.

3

If you connect Google Calendar here, those candidate dates are cross-checked against your real calendar — only truly-free dates are used for matching.

4

Band Suggestions shows every band that is available on those dates AND meets at least 2 of your venue's preferences.

5

When an agent creates a booking, a gig event is added to your Google Calendar automatically.
