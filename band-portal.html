ℹ️ How It Works

1
Click Connect with Google and sign in with the Google account that owns your calendar.
2
Grant permission for Dave Kalz Agency to add events to your calendar.
3
Whenever an agent creates a booking for your band, a gig event is automatically added to your Google Calendar with the venue, date, and time.
4
You can disconnect at any time. Existing calendar events will remain but new bookings will no longer be added automatically.
